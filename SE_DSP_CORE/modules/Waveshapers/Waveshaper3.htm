Waveshapers

Distorts the input depending on a transfer function you draw by dragging the control points with your mouse. Can be used as a distortion unit, soft clipper, waveform modifier, or control voltage transfer function (e.g. custom velocity response curve).
  NOTE: If the center node is not exactly in the center of the grid, an input of zero (silence) will tranform into a DC-Offset (non-zero constant output).  This may cause audible clicks in your signal.
